BigRing Cycling - TNT

Weekly Criterium/Circuit Race Practice! Have fun racing with fellow cyclists that love to go fast. All levels welcome from elite to just-getting-started. Masters, Womens and Novice heats are 2 laps, Open is 4 laps

BigRing Cycling is dedicated to providing a supportive and safe environment for cyclists of all levels to learn and practice the art of racing. Come join us!
